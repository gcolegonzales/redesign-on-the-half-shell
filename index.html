Since the early 2000s

A neighborhood oyster bar that never lost the beat.

For over twenty years, On The Half Shell has been Prairieville's spot for fresh Louisiana seafood and a good time. We serve daily lunch specials, a full lunch and dinner menu, Sunday brunch, and live music Tuesday through Saturday evenings — plus Sunday during brunch.

Everything is dressed the Louisiana way, from the raw bar out front to the boil in back. Pull up a stool, grab a cold one, and stay for the band.
